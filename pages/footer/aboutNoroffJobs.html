About Noroff jobs

Welcome to Noroff Jobs - a unique intersection between education and the professional world, developed and operated by students at Noroff. As a non-profit organization, our mission is to bridge the gap between academia and the job market, ensuring that our students have valuable opportunities to apply their skills in the real world.

For students and companies

For students, Noroff Jobs is your direct access to the industry. Meet your next employer today and showcase your unique skill sets, whether in software development, design, or security. Our platform provides you with an exclusive opportunity to discover your dream job, with listings from a wide variety of organizations. We are committed to being your partner in achieving your career goals. For companies, Noroff Jobs offers a unique advantage over other recruitment platforms. As a platform designed, developed, and maintained by students at Noroff, it provides direct access to a talented pool of candidates with diverse skillsets. We invite companies to submit their job listings for free and partner with Noroff Jobs to expand their team with highly skilled and motivated individuals.

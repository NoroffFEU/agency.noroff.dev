Company User Guide

Welcome to the Noroff Jobs Company User Guide. This guide will help you navigate our platform and effectively post job listings to connect with talented students and graduates.

Creating an Account

To get started, register for an account using your company email. Once verified, you will have access to our job posting features.

Posting a Job Listing

- Navigate to the "Create Listing" section.
- Fill out the job details, including title, description, and required qualifications.
- Set an application deadline.
- Upload any relevant media or documents.
- Submit for review before publishing.

Managing Applications

Once your job listing is live, you can track and manage applications through your dashboard. Shortlist candidates and communicate with them directly through the platform.
